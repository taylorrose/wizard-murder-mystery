Minister Aldric Thorne

Acting Minister of Wonders

Background: Appointed six months ago after the previous Minister's mysterious disappearance. Known for implementing controversial transparency reforms.

Personality: Ambitious, reform-minded, makes enemies among traditionalists.

Key Relationships: Works closely with Commander Keats; often at odds with Inquisitor March; old friend of Curator Bales.

Areas of Authority: Policy decisions, high-level clearances, interdepartmental coordination.

×

Commander Brina Keats

Head of Magical Law Enforcement

Background: Decorated Auror with 15 years of service. Rose through ranks after solving the Goblin Vault conspiracy.

Personality: Methodical, loyal, by-the-book investigator.

Key Relationships: Reports to Minister Thorne; oversees Agent Blackwood; respects Archivist Vane.

Areas of Expertise: Security protocols, defensive magic, criminal investigation.

×

Archivist Nella Vane

Keeper of Magical Records

Background: Maintains vast archives of magical contracts, genealogies, and historical records. Has eidetic memory.

Personality: Meticulous, knowledgeable, protective of information.

Special Ability: Perfect Recall - can remember any document ever read.

Areas of Knowledge: Employee records, historical precedents, magical lineages.

×

Healer Corwin Dredge

Chief Medi-Wizard

Background: Ministry's chief medical officer, responsible for employee health and forensic examination.

Personality: Compassionate, bound by medical ethics, family-oriented.

Tonight's Role: Performed preliminary examination of the victim.

Areas of Expertise: Magical maladies, potion identification, cause of death analysis.

×

Inquisitor Selwyn March

Internal Affairs Investigator

Background: Investigates corruption and misconduct within the Ministry itself.

Personality: Suspicious of everyone, ruthless pursuit of truth, makes people nervous.

Current Focus: Building cases against potential Ministry corruption.

Reputation: Both respected and feared for uncompromising investigations.

×

Magizoologist Elara Finn

Creature Behavior Specialist

Background: Studies magical creatures and their applications in magic.

Personality: Enthusiastic about creatures, protective of research, somewhat scattered.

Special Access: Rare creature specimens including metamorphmagus samples.

Areas of Knowledge: Creature-based potion ingredients, behavioral patterns.

×

Curator Theon Bales

Keeper of Magical Artifacts

Background: Manages the Ministry's collection of powerful magical artifacts.

Personality: Nervous, eager to please, worried about reputation.

Key Item: Oversees the Mirror of Masks (projects false appearances).

Connection: Old friend of Minister Thorne who helped secure position.

×

Correspondent Lyra Quill

Prophet Investigative Journalist

Background: Investigative reporter for the Daily Prophet, here for exclusive Ministry tour.

Personality: Persistent, observant, always seeking the story.

Motivation: Wants to expose Ministry corruption and cover-ups.

Reputation: Known for hard-hitting exposés and protecting sources.

×

Wandwright Cassian Thorn

Master Wand Craftsman

Background: Expert in wand creation, maintenance, and analysis.

Personality: Precise, secretive about clients, protective of craft.

Special Skill: Can identify wands by their magical signatures.

Key Knowledge: Understands wand tracking and modification techniques.

×

Unspeakable Dorian Mire

Department of Mysteries Researcher

Background: Works in the Hall of Time on classified temporal research.

Personality: Cryptic, bound by secrecy oaths, speaks in riddles.

Constraints: Cannot discuss most of their work due to classification.

Specialty: Time magic, temporal anomalies, paradox prevention.

×

Enchanter Vexa Rune

Spell Development Specialist

Background: Creates and tests new spells for Ministry use.

Personality: Brilliant, ambitious, wants recognition for work.

Recent Project: Developed an unmasking charm on commission.

Expertise: Spell mechanics, magical theory, charm detection.

×

Agent Soren Blackwood

Special Operations Liaison

Background: Handles sensitive missions between departments. Five years of exemplary service.

Personality: Professional, secretive, deflects personal questions.

Expertise: Undercover work, infiltration, counter-intelligence.

Note: Much of their work is classified and cannot be discussed.
